About Question Paper Generator

Welcome to the Question Paper Generator, a software designed to help teachers and educators create fair and unbiased question papers for their students. Our software uses a database of questions and algorithms to generate unique question papers for each student, ensuring that no two students receive the same question paper.

How to Use Question Paper Generator

To use our software, simply follow these steps:

  1. Create a question bank by adding, editing, or deleting questions as needed.
  2. Select the subject, topic, and difficulty level for the question paper.
  3. Click the "Generate Question Paper" button to create a unique question paper for each student.
  4. Customize the question paper by adding or removing questions, or by changing the difficulty level.
  5. Save the question paper to the database, along with the other information entered by the user, such as the question type, answer options, and correct answer.
